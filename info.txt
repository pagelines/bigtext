== Description ==

Wish FitText could put more than one word on a line? Come enjoy the benefits of the robust BigText Section!

<h3>Highlights</h3>
<p style="padding-left:30px;">&raquo; Up to 9 separate lines of text that all scale up or down to fit the area
<br />&raquo; Each line can have its own color with the built-in color picker
<br />&raquo; Each line can have the BigText effect "turned off" (called "exempt") with a simple checkbox
<br />&raquo; Pick your own BigText font
<br />&raquo; Pick a color or image background (optional transparent text)
<br />&raquo; Customize the width of the BigText area, and it's responsive
<br />&raquo; Choose your own CSS line-height, margin, text-align values
<br />&raquo; Specify your maximum font-size in pixels (default is 528px)
<br />&raquo; Specify your minimum font-size in pixels (default is no minimum)
<br />&raquo; And more!</p>
